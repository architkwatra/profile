About Me

Hi! My name is Archit Kwatra. I am a second year Masters student at the Department of Computer Science, North Carolina State University. Since my childhood, I have been fascinated by the power of a computer and with time my fascination turned into curiousness which led me here. My research interests are Human Computer Interaction and Reinforcement Learning. I am looking for full time opportunities where I could mix my research interests with the conventional software engineering practices to deliver standalone, simple and useful solutions to users. I am currently a part of the HINTS Lab (at NCSU) and working with Dr. Thomas W. Price.


I have done my undergraduate studies from Guru Gobind Singh Indraprastha University (2012-2016) in Electronics and Communications. Prior to joining NC State University I have worked as a Software Engineer at TAIStech (india), a Dallas based organisation, for two and a half years where my primary focus was to develop e-commerce web applications using Javscript/TypeScript. I was responsible for end-to-end development of a couple of e-commerce web applications: DSW and DXL. I am also interested in learning mobile application development and I do posess a basic understanding in iOS application development.
